About US

Brighten Education is an education service founded by senior educators and IT specialists in Brighton Victoria, providing multi-faceted multi-dimensional educational services, i.e. academic tutoring, English language competence acceleration, hobby development, and overall ability enhancement. As its name suggests, BE is dedicated to wising up its students and accompanying and assisting them on their way of BEing.

For this purpose, BE has congregated Australian renowned educators as its advisors’ panel and highly experienced teachers as its teachers’ pool, complemented by meticulous scientific administration by savvy education management team, and is fast moving toward being a mentor for students, a taker of parenting burden, and a catalyst for familial assimilation in Australia.

Teaching philosophy

  • READING CLUB

    80% of human knowledge is obtained from reading, The development of reading capability is a gradual process started form early childhood. While reading is the foundation of learning and education, early childhood reading is the core of that foundation.

  • ROBOT CLASS

    Robot education caters to children’s love of palying,thus can guide children to learn under a joyful atmosphere. What’s more, it breaks the boundares of conventional disciplines and helps children to develop multiple capabilities in the process of accomplishing tasks, and improves their creative thinking.

  • ENGLISH CLASS

    A professional tutoring institution can help newly arrived children to get improvement in listening, speaking, reading, and writing in a short period of time so that they can quickly integrate into local cultural of living and learning.

  • GRAMMAR & VOCAB

    Vocabulary is the foundation of English learning and grammar is the basic of any language. Language is a complete system, of which grammar is the rule. Communication skills and logical thinking and based on mastering grammar as well.

  • SCHOLARSHIP

    Proper goal setting is the starting point for children’s self-management. Setting specific, highly operable goals for them, with scholarships will fully motivate children to learn.

  • WRITING

    For most children, English writing is the weakest aspect. Regular and systematic writing exercises will improve children’s logical thinking as well as expressing skills.

Teaching Philosophy

It is a deeply-rooted belief of Brighten Education that there are no two identical students. In consideration of the differences in learning habit, skill set, goals and family background of students and the varied expectations and level of supportiveness of their parents, BE would provide customised learning plans for each student and closely follow them up to help students achieve the best learning result and grow healthily and happily.

The educational philosophy of BE originates from the internationally prevailing idea of STEAM, i.e. Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics. A fusion of STEAM and the Australian curriculum will nourish our students with such core qualities as problem-solving skills, logical thinking capacity and immense creativity. Conceived in the proposition of “using what is learnt to solve problems”, students will find learning at BE means interdisciplinary integration of arts, science and more in an immersive fashion.
